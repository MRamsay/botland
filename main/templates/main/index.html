Thesis

My thesis comprised of adding new features for an online catalogue of supernova remnants, SNRcat. I made a series of radio, X-ray, and gamma ray images for each supernova remnant in our Galaxy. Along with the images, I did a comprehensive literature study to get detailed brightness values in X-ray for all Galactic supernova remnants, along with many other associated properties. Those brightness values are yet to be published on SNRcat, but when it's made public it should be a great asset for researchers.

I also made interactive maps of the locations of supernova remnants, and generally improved the website.

You can see my thesis here if you're interested in the details. It looks huge but the meat of it is only around 30 pages and an easy read, the other 470 pages is supplemental material.
